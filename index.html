FAQ

  • Q: How do I import a video?
    A: Open the app and tap Import Video to pick from your Camera Roll.
  • Q: How do I split a video?
    A: After importing, set your desired split length and tap Split.
  • Q: How do I export the results?
    A: Tap Export to save the split videos to your Camera Roll or share them.
